Nvidia: Two Clocks

发表于 2026-02-28 更新于 2026-03-01

This is part of a series on the Magnificent Seven. The framing — four distinct scenarios, seven companies that should not be priced as one story — is laid out in the original memo.

本文是科技七巨头系列的一部分。四种截然不同的终局剧本——完整框架见原始备忘录。

Nvidia just reported Q4 FY2026: $68.1 billion in total revenue, $62.3 billion from data centers, up 75% year-over-year. Q1 FY2027 guidance is $78 billion — $5 billion above analyst consensus. The stock barely moved. At a trailing PE of ~48x and forward PE of ~30x, the market has two concerns running simultaneously that it treats as one. They are not one. They operate on entirely different timescales, have different triggers, and require different hedges.

英伟达刚刚公布了 FY2026 第四季度业绩：总收入 681 亿美元，数据中心收入 623 亿美元，同比增长 75%。FY2027 第一季度指引 780 亿美元——高出分析师共识 50 亿。股价几乎没动。以约 48 倍的追踪市盈率和约 30 倍的远期市盈率，市场同时怀揣着两种担忧，却把它们当成一件事来定价。它们不是一件事，运行在完全不同的时间尺度上，有不同的触发机制，需要不同的对冲策略。
