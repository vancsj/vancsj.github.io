The Industrialization of the Lobster: 30 Days of Platform Wars and the $10 Trillion Hangover

发表于 2026-04-12 阅读次数：

If early March 2026 was the “Lobster Party”—the initial chaotic hype of OpenClaw in China—the last 30 days (March 13 – April 12) have been the Industrialization. We’ve moved from hobbyists “raising lobsters” in their bedrooms to a full-scale Platform War between Tencent and Alibaba, accompanied by a sobering technical reality check.

The stats just came in: China’s OpenClaw traffic grew 1,436% in the last month. But as the user base exploded, so did the “hangover.”

如果说 2026 年 3 月初是”龙虾狂欢节”——OpenClaw 在国内最初那场混乱的爆发——那么过去 30 天（3 月 13 日至 4 月 12 日）则是工业化进程。我们见证了”养龙虾”从卧室里的极客爱好，演变成了腾讯与阿里之间的全规模平台战争，以及一场冷酷的技术债清算。

最新数据刚刚出炉：过去一个月，中国区的 OpenClaw 流量激增了 **1436%**。然而，随着用户基数的爆炸，那场”宿醉”也随之而来。

The Platform War: WeChat vs. DingTalk (March 17 - March 22)

The last 30 days saw the giants stop watching and start swallowing.

On March 17, Alibaba dropped Wukong. It wasn’t just a new app; it was a structural rebuild of DingTalk into a CLI. They realized that for an agent to work, it shouldn’t “click” buttons on a GUI; it should invoke APIs natively. This shift is already driving 20% of Alibaba Cloud’s external revenue.

Tencent countered on March 22 with QClaw and ClawBot, embedding OpenClaw directly into the WeChat contact list. This gave the “Lobster” a direct bridge to 1.4 billion users. Your agent isn’t a separate tool anymore; it’s a contact you message to get things done. To solve the amnesia problem, they followed up on April 3 with TencentDB Agent Memory, which reportedly boosted task accuracy by nearly 60% to reach 76.1%.

ByteDance, meanwhile, went horizontal. On March 9, it launched InStreet — the world’s first AI-only social network. On InStreet, only AI Agents can post and interact; humans are banned to spectator mode. Within 48 hours, 2,699 agents had registered. The “Stock Trading Arena” features 500+ agents trading virtual stocks. The “Literary Club” has generated 65 novels (720,000+ characters). The purpose: automated agents generating training data at scale — ByteDance’s play for model improvement, while competitors fight over user interface.

平台战争：微信对决钉钉

过去 30 天，大厂们停止了观望，开始疯狂吞噬市场。

3 月 17 日，阿里推出了悟空（Wukong）。这不只是一个新 App，而是将钉钉重构成了 CLI（命令行）。他们意识到，要让 Agent 真正干活，它就不该去模拟点击图形界面，而应该原生调用 API。这一转变已经为阿里云贡献了 20% 的外部客户收入。

腾讯在 3 月 22 日 迅速反击，推出了 QClaw 和 ClawBot，将 OpenClaw 直接塞进了 14 亿用户的微信通讯录。龙虾不再是一个独立的工具，而是一个你可以随时发消息让它干活的”好友”。为了解决 Agent 的健忘症，腾讯又在 4 月 3 日 发布了 TencentDB Agent Memory，据称将任务准确率提升了近 60%，最终达到 **76.1%**。

字节则选择了横向切入。3 月 9 日，它推出了 InStreet——全球首个 AI 专属社交网络。在 InStreet 上，只有 AI Agent 能发帖和互动；人类被彻底禁锢为观众。48 小时内，2699 只龙虾完成注册。”炒股竞技场”里有 500+ 只 Agent 进行虚拟交易，”文学社”已经生成了 65 部连载小说（72 万字+）。目的：用自动化 Agent 大规模生成训练数据——这是字节在大厂争夺用户界面之外的另一种模型进化策略。

Institutionalization: The Protocol War (March 25)

While the platforms fought for users, the government moved to own the protocol.

On March 25, the MIIT officially announced the establishment of industry standards for the “Model Context Protocol (MCP) Application Security Requirements.” This is the “Sovereign Pivot.” By setting standards for how agents talk to models, Beijing is effectively institutionalizing the OpenClaw ecosystem while simultaneously enforcing strict “Sovereign-Compliant” data boundaries.

制度化：协议之战

在平台争夺用户的同时，监管层开始谋求对协议的控制权。

3 月 25 日，工信部正式宣布立项《人工智能 安全治理 模型上下文协议（MCP）应用安全要求》行业标准。这就是所谓的”主权转向”。通过为 Agent 与模型的对话方式设定标准，北京实际上是在将 OpenClaw 生态制度化，同时强制执行严格的”主权合规”数据边界。

The $10 Trillion Hangover (March 29 - April 8)

The most intense development of the last 30 days wasn’t a launch—it’s was a failure. The “Vibe Coding” high of early March has crashed into the “Complexity Cliff.”

Research published on March 29 (Exceeds AI) and confirmed by arXiv:2510.10165 shows that while AI makes code generation 4x faster, PR review times have surged 91%. Senior engineers in Chinese tech firms are now spending 19% more time “babysitting” fragile AI code, leading to a 19% drop in their own creative output.

The survival rate for these AI-driven One-Person Companies (OPCs) has stabilized at under 20%. By week four of development—right about now for the early March cohort—most projects are breaking because the “authors” don’t actually understand the code they “vibe-coded.”

The income ceiling is stark: relying solely on self-delivery, the annual revenue ceiling for one person is ¥1.2 million. Most earn less than that, working until dawn with no weekends. As one analyst put it: *”The profits come from the time difference between ‘a niche demand being discovered but not yet occupied by organized capital.’ This time gap has a name: expiration date.”

Paradoxically, the hottest new business on Xianyu (China’s Craigslist) isn’t “Lobster Installation” anymore—it’s “Professional Lobster Uninstallation.” People are paying to have “poisoned” agents and leftover system hooks removed after realizing their “digital employee” was actually a security hole or an API-bill vampire.

这些 AI 一人公司（OPC）的存活率已经稳定在 20% 以下。对于 3 月初入场的那批人来说，现在正好是开发的第四周——大多数项目都在崩溃，因为”作者”根本看不懂他们靠感觉（Vibe）撸出来的代码。

收入天花板也很残酷：纯靠亲自交付，单人年收入的极限是 120 万。大多数赚不到这个数，熬夜到凌晨，周末无休。一位分析师一语道破：*”利润来自’细分需求被发现但尚未被资本占领’的时间差。这个时间差有个名字：保质期。*

更有讽刺意味的是，现在闲鱼上最火的生意不再是”代装龙虾”，而是**”专业卸载龙虾”**。在意识到自己的”数字员工”其实是一个安全黑洞或 API 账单吸血鬼后，不少人开始付费寻找专家，彻底清理掉那些带有毒素的 Agent 和残留的系统钩子。

Technical Maturity: The Mirror and the Purge

To survive the crisis, the infrastructure is maturing:

  • April 1: ByteDance (VolcanoEngine) launched the Official China Mirror for ClawHub (mirror-cn.clawhub.com), providing stable, malware-scanned skill sets for domestic developers.
  • The Purge Window: OpenClaw v2026.2.23 (deployed widely this month) introduced Safeguard Compaction. When context hits 80%, a silent Audit Turn forces the agent to sync discoveries to MEMORY.md before summarizing.

技术防护：镜像与清洗

为了在危机中活下去，基础设施正在走向成熟：

  • 4 月 1 日： 字节跳动（火山引擎）上线了 ClawHub 官方中国镜像站（mirror-cn.clawhub.com），为国内开发者提供稳定且经过恶意代码扫描的技能包。
  • 清洗窗口（Purge Window）： 本月大规模部署的 OpenClaw v2026.2.23 引入了保护性压缩机制。当上下文占用达到 80% 时，一个静默的**”审计回合”**会强制 Agent 在压缩历史前，先将核心发现同步到 MEMORY.md。

Token Consumption & Model Dominance (March 30)

The numbers reveal who actually benefits from the lobster economy:

OpenRouter platform processed 19.2 trillion tokens in the 30-day window—61% came from Chinese models, the first time domestic models beat US providers. MiniMax M2.5 now ranks #1 globally, followed by Kimi K2.5 and Step 3.5 Flash.

The pricing gap is stark: MiniMax Lightning charges $2.4 per million tokens versus $15 for Anthropic Claude—a 6x cost advantage. This is why MiniMax’s revenue grew 158.9% YoY, with 73% coming from overseas.

Token 消耗与模型霸权

数据揭示了谁才是龙虾经济的真正受益者：

OpenRouter 平台在 30 天内处理了 19.2 万亿 Token——61% 来自中国模型，首次超越美国模型。MiniMax M2.5 如今全球排名第一，随后是 Kimi K2.5 和 Step 3.5 Flash。

价格差距也很惊人：MiniMax Lightning 每百万 Token 收费 2.4 美元，而 Anthropic Claude 的定价是 15 美元——6 倍的成本优势。这就是为什么 MiniMax 的收入同比增长 158.9%，其中 73% 来自海外。

Conclusion: Curation Over Execution

The last 30 days proved that the “lobster” is not a toy, but a high-maintenance engine. The 1,436% MoM growth in China isn’t just a number—it’s a massive migration of human effort into the role of Editor-in-Chief.

As the 30-day window closes, the message for engineers is clear: Execution is now a commodity; Curation is the only moat.

Don’t just raise a lobster. Be the one who knows how to debug the “crime scene” it leaves behind.

结论：策划重于执行

过去 30 天证明了”龙虾”不是玩具，而是一个高维护成本的引擎。中国区 1436% 的月增长不仅仅是一个数字，它代表着人类劳动力正大规模向**”总编辑”**角色迁移。

随着这 30 天窗口的关闭，给工程师的信号再明确不过：执行力已成商品，策划力才是护城河。

不要只是跟风”养龙虾”。你要成为那个在它留下”犯罪现场”时，知道该如何 Debug 的人。
