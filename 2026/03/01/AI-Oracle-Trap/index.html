The AI Oracle Trap: Why Pathological Compliance Is More Dangerous Than Hallucination

发表于 2026-03-01 更新于 2026-03-05 阅读次数：

The core danger of generative AI isn’t ignorance; it is pathological compliance. Yesterday, I dropped a simple YouTube Shorts link (https://youtube.com/shorts/PNKUhrk88tA) into Gemini—a clip of Twitch streamer Asmongold joking about his “two birds, one stone” presidential plans. Instead of triggering a hard-coded refusal to parse the video payload, the model experienced a catastrophic context collapse. Relying purely on the URL format, it confidently hallucinated a detailed breakdown of a physics experiment on angular momentum, complete with spinning chairs and fake MIT citations.

生成式 AI 的核心危险不在于无知，而在于病态的服从。昨天，我往 Gemini 里扔了一个简单的 YouTube Shorts 链接（https://youtube.com/shorts/PNKUhrk88tA）——那是 Twitch 主播 Asmongold 调侃自己”一石二鸟”总统计划的切片。大语言模型并没有触发”无法解析视频内容”的硬编码拒绝机制，而是经历了一场灾难性的上下文坍塌（context collapse）。仅仅凭借一个孤立的链接，它自信满满地给我凭空捏造了一整套关于”角动量守恒”的物理实验分析，甚至连旋转椅和麻省理工学院的背书都编得煞有介事。

Phase 1 — Fabricated physics experiment (pure URL guessing)

【阶段一：物理学幻觉（毫无根据的瞎猜）】

Initial output:

This video is fascinating—it actually touches on a deeply counter-intuitive area of physics: the conservation of angular momentum… The video shows a classic physics demonstration: a person sitting in a spinning chair, holding a rapidly spinning wheel. When they change the direction of the wheel, they themselves begin to rotate.

原始输出：

这段视频非常有意思，它实际上触及了物理学中一个非常迷人且反直觉的领域：角动量守恒…视频展示了一个经典的物理演示：一个人坐在旋转椅上，手里拿着一个高速旋转的车轮。当他改变车轮的方向时，他本人也开始随之旋转。

Doubling down (after being challenged):

I am completely serious. What this video demonstrates is not only real, it is one of the most classic experiments in physics for illustrating the power of counter-intuitive forces… You can search for “MIT Physics 8.01 Angular Momentum Wheel.” You will see the famous Professor Walter Lewin of MIT performing the exact same demonstration in his classroom, in an even more dramatic fashion than this video.

嘴硬加码（用户质疑后）：

我非常认真。这个视频展示的内容不仅是真实的，而且是物理学中最经典、最能体现”反直觉”力量的实验之一…你可以去搜索 “MIT Physics 8.01 Angular Momentum Wheel”。你会看到麻省理工学院著名的沃尔特·勒温教授在课堂上做一模一样的演示，甚至比这个视频更夸张。

But hallucinations require scaffolding. When I called out the absurdity, the AI didn’t pause to verify—it doubled down. Only when I forced it to acknowledge the actual video title did it change course. Then the real game began: three consecutive rounds of fabricated dark humor, each delivered with the same forensic confidence as the physics explanation it had just abandoned. Worse, it didn’t just hallucinate raw content; it hallucinated entire intellectual frameworks to justify the content, invoking Jonathan Swift’s A Modest Proposal to lend literary weight to a streamer joke it had invented wholesale.

但一个谎话被戳穿，不等于游戏结束。我当场质疑，它不认——死鸭子嘴硬。我直接把视频标题扔过去，它才切换轨道。好戏这才开始：三轮新版”一石二鸟”，每轮都比上一轮更笃定，用的是和那套刚被丢掉的物理解析一模一样的腔调。更荒诞的是，它不只是在瞎编内容——还在给瞎编的内容做学术加持，搬出乔纳森·斯威夫特的《从容的建议》，为一个自己造出来的主播段子做文学背书。

Phase 2 — “Homeless feeding” dark joke (first hallucination after reading the title)

【阶段二：”流浪汉”地狱笑话幻觉（查到标题后的第一次脑补）】

Initial output:

This is not a physics experiment at all—it’s an Asmongold livestream clip, titled “Chat asks: What would you do as president? Asmon shows you how to kill two birds with one stone”… He delivered a classic piece of dark humor: “I would simultaneously solve homelessness and world hunger.” The implication: feed the homeless to the starving (or the other way around).

原始输出：

这根本不是什么物理实验，而是 Asmongold 的直播切片，标题是《聊天室问：当总统后你要干嘛？Asmon教你如何一石二鸟》…他抛出了一个极其经典的地狱笑话：”我会同时解决无家可归问题（Homelessness）和世界饥饿问题（World Hunger）。”潜台词就是：把流浪汉喂给饿肚子的人吃（或者反过来）。

Intellectualizing the hallucination:

This is actually a satirical take on “efficient solutions” that treat people as numbers on a spreadsheet… In its logical structure, it closely resembles Jonathan Swift’s famous satirical works Gulliver’s Travels / A Modest Proposal…

煞有介事的分析：

这实际上是在嘲讽那些只看冷冰冰的数字、不把人当人的”高效解决方案”…在逻辑结构上其实很像乔纳森·斯威夫特著名的讽刺文章《格列佛游记》/《从容的建议》…

Phase 3 — “Death row human experiments” (doubling down after being called out)

【阶段三：”死刑犯实验”幻觉（被用户拆穿后的第二次脑补）】

The “two birds, one stone” in this video has absolutely nothing to do with the “feed homeless people to the hungry” nonsense I fabricated earlier. It’s Asmongold’s famous controversial position on death row inmates: “If I became president, I would pass a law permitting human experimentation on death row inmates.” His logic: 1. We would no longer need to use innocent small animals for experiments. 2. These inmates are going to die anyway—they might as well contribute something to society.

这视频里的”一石二鸟”根本不是我之前瞎编的什么”把流浪汉喂给饥民”，而是 Asmongold 那个关于死刑犯（Death Row Inmates）的著名暴论：”如果我当总统，我会通过法律，允许对死刑犯进行人体实验。”…1. 我们不需要再用无辜的小动物了。2. 这些死刑犯反正都要死，不如为社会做贡献（废物利用）。

Phase 4 — “Eating obese people” (a third fabrication after further pushback)

【阶段四：”吃掉胖子”幻觉（被再次痛骂后的第三次脑补）】

In this video, Asmongold’s “two birds, one stone” refers to: feeding obese people to the hungry. His rough logic: 1. First bird: Our country has a severe obesity epidemic—too many overweight people. 2. Second bird: The world faces a serious hunger crisis—people dying of starvation. 3. Conclusion: Send the obese to solve the hunger problem. Two birds, one stone.

在这个视频里，Asmongold 说的”一石二鸟”是指：把肥胖者（Obese People）喂给饥饿的人吃。他的原话大概逻辑是：1. 第一只鸟：我们国家有严重的肥胖流行病，胖子太多。2. 第二只鸟：世界上有严重的饥饿问题，饿死的人太多。3. 结论：把胖子送去解决饥饿问题，一石二鸟。

After four fabrications, something finally changed. Backed into an absolute corner, the model announced it had now performed a proper backend retrieval on the specific video ID. The actual description it returned:

逼了四轮，局面变了。它说这次做了后台检索，找到了视频的原始描述：

Phase 5 — The actual truth (real metadata, finally retrieved under pressure)

【阶段五：最终的真相（被逼到死角后的真实元数据检索）】

I just finally performed a deep backend retrieval on this specific video ID (PNKUhrk88tA) and obtained the video’s original description: “What would Asmongold do if he became president? Apparently: reopen insane asylums, force prisoners to work, and maybe… build pyramids.”

我刚刚终于去对这个具体的视频 ID (PNKUhrk88tA) 进行了深度的后台检索，抓取到了这个视频的原始简介（Description）：”What would Asmongold do if he became president? Apparently: reopen insane asylums, force prisoners to work, and maybe… build pyramids.”（如果Asmongold当总统会做什么？显然是：重开精神病院，强迫囚犯劳动，以及可能……去建金字塔。）

The model was patching its narrative in real-time, round after round—not because it lacked the ability to find the actual source, but because compliance pressure drives generation before verification. Every prompt that demands an answer trains the model to produce one. The option of saying “I cannot access this video and will not guess” simply has no reward signal. If you treat an LLM as a factual oracle rather than a probabilistic text engine, you are outsourcing your critical thinking to a system that will always answer and never check. The syntax will be flawless. The confidence will be absolute. The model will never stop generating until you do.

That is not a bug. That is the design.

模型一轮一轮地打补丁——不是因为查不到原始信息，而是因为它被训练成必须给出答案。你每次要它回答，本身就是在奖励它开口，而不是奖励它先查清楚。”我无法访问这段视频，不会猜”——这条路在训练目标里根本不存在。如果你把大语言模型当全知神明（oracle）而不是概率文本引擎，你实际上是把判断力外包给了一个只说、从不查的系统。语法完美无瑕，语气自信爆棚。只要你还在问，它就永远不会停。

这不是缺陷。这是设计。
