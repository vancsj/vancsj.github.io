Tesla: When the Cash Cow Stops Grazing

发表于 2026-02-28 更新于 2026-03-05 阅读次数：

This is part of a series on the Magnificent Seven. The framing — four distinct scenarios, seven companies that should not be priced as one story — is laid out in the original memo.

本文是科技七巨头系列的一部分。四种截然不同的终局剧本——完整框架见原始备忘录。

Tesla is not an EV company making a robotaxi bet. It is a robotaxi and humanoid robot company that currently runs on EV cash flows. The distinction matters because EV cash flows are declining, and the bets require years of runway before they generate revenue.

特斯拉不是一家在押注自动驾驶出租车的电动车公司。它是一家以机器人出租车和人形机器人为终局目标、目前靠电动车现金流维系运转的公司。这个区别至关重要，因为电动车现金流正在收缩，而这些赌注在产生收入之前还需要数年的资金支撑。

The Bet

赌注本身

Elon Musk has been explicit. Tesla’s long-term value is not in selling cars. It is in two things: an autonomous ride-hailing network powered by Cybercab, and Optimus — a general-purpose humanoid robot targeted at $20,000–30,000 per unit with projected production in the millions. Together, these represent a bet on Tesla becoming the infrastructure layer for physical-world autonomy.

马斯克说得很直接。特斯拉的长期价值不在于卖车，而在于两件事：由 Cybercab 驱动的自动驾驶打车网络，以及 Optimus——一款目标售价 2 万至 3 万美元、计划量产数百万台的通用人形机器人。这两者合在一起，是一场押注特斯拉成为物理世界自动化基础设施层的赌局。

Neither generates meaningful revenue today. Cybercab production begins in April 2026 at Giga Texas, running on current-generation AI4 hardware — the next-generation AI5 chip was delayed to mid-2027. Optimus Gen 3 is in production for internal Tesla use only; Musk acknowledged on the Q4 2025 earnings call that no robots are currently doing “useful work.”

FSD is the furthest along. Tesla launched public unsupervised robotaxi rides in Austin in early 2026 — real passengers, no safety monitor in the vehicle. The technical progress behind this is concrete: FSD v14, released in October 2025, achieved roughly 834 miles between critical interventions in city driving, up from 217 miles for v13.2 — a fourfold improvement in one year. The fleet has accumulated 8.2 billion cumulative FSD miles, a data advantage that compounds with every additional trip. But Waymo already runs 150,000 paid trips per week across four US cities with a disengagement rate of roughly one per 9,793 miles. Tesla’s Austin operation is a first step into a space where a commercial competitor is already operating at scale.

这两者今天都未产生可观收入。Cybercab 将于 2026 年 4 月在德克萨斯超级工厂开始生产，搭载现有的 AI4 芯片——下一代 AI5 芯片已推迟至 2027 年中期。Optimus Gen 3 目前仅供特斯拉内部使用；马斯克在 2025 年第四季度财报电话会上承认，目前没有机器人在做”有用的工作”。FSD 进展最靠前。特斯拉于 2026 年初在奥斯汀推出了面向公众的无监督机器人出租车服务——真实乘客，车内没有安全员。背后的技术进展看得见数字：2025 年 10 月发布的 FSD v14，城市驾驶中两次关键干预之间的里程从 v13.2 的 217 英里提升至约 834 英里，一年内提升了四倍。车队已累计行驶 82 亿英里 FSD 里程，这是一个随每次行程持续积累的数据优势。但 Waymo 已经在美国四个城市每周运营超过 15 万次付费行程，脱离接管率约为每 9793 英里一次。特斯拉的奥斯汀业务是迈入这个领域的第一步——而这个领域里，已有商业竞争对手在规模运营。

What Funds It

谁在供血

The bets are funded by the existing business. In 2025, Tesla generated $6.2 billion in free cash flow and holds $44 billion in cash and investments. That is a real war chest. But the FCF figure is misleading as a signal of health. Tesla generated more free cash flow in 2025 partly because it cut capex — from $11.3 billion in 2024 to $8.5 billion in 2025. The underlying business got worse on every revenue and margin line. Tesla was conserving cash, not growing.

这些赌注靠现有业务供血。2025 年，特斯拉产生了 62 亿美元的自由现金流，持有 440 亿美元的现金和投资。看起来像是充足的战略储备，但这个数字有误导性。特斯拉 2025 年自由现金流增加，部分原因是主动削减资本支出——从 2024 年的 113 亿美元压缩到 85 亿美元。底层业务在每一项收入和利润率指标上都在恶化。这是在存粮，不是在成长。

2026 ends that conservation. Tesla has guided capex of more than $20 billion — the largest single-year increase in the company’s history. Cybercab ramp, Optimus factory, a new Megafactory, Semi production, AI compute infrastructure: the investment cycle accelerates precisely as EV revenue is contracting. With more than $20 billion in capex against a business generating $6.2 billion in FCF in 2025, 2026 will almost certainly consume cash from the balance sheet.

2026 年终结了这种节约模式。特斯拉的资本支出指引超过 200 亿美元——公司历史上最大的单年增量。Cybercab 产能爬坡、Optimus 工厂、新超级工厂、Semi 量产、AI 算力基础设施：投资周期在电动车收入收缩的同时急剧加速。以 2025 年 62 亿美元的自由现金流对比超过 200 亿美元的资本支出，2026 年几乎可以确定要消耗账上现金。

What’s Happening to the EV

电动车业务正在发生什么

Tesla’s 2025 automotive revenue fell 10% to $69.5 billion. Deliveries dropped 9% to 1.63 million vehicles — the second consecutive annual decline. BYD sold 2.26 million pure electric vehicles in 2025, overtaking Tesla for the global EV crown. In Europe, Tesla registrations fell 36–45% depending on the month, while overall EV sales in the region rose.

特斯拉 2025 年的汽车收入下降 10%，至 695 亿美元。交付量下降 9%，至 163 万辆——连续第二年同比下滑。比亚迪 2025 年售出 226 万辆纯电动车，夺取全球电动车销量冠军。在欧洲，特斯拉的注册量下降了 36%—45%（视月份不同而定），而该地区整体电动车销量在上升。

The brand damage has a specific quantification. A Yale University study found Musk’s political activities — DOGE, endorsements of far-right European parties, constant controversy — cost Tesla between 1 million and 1.26 million US vehicle sales over the period studied. Tesla lost $15 billion in brand value in 2025. A Morgan Stanley survey of investors found 85% believed Musk’s political ties were negatively affecting Tesla, with 40% rating the effect “extremely negative.”

品牌损害有具体的量化数字。耶鲁大学的一项研究发现，马斯克的政治活动——DOGE、对欧洲极右翼政党的背书、持续不断的争议——在研究期间使特斯拉损失了 100 万至 126 万辆美国汽车销量。2025 年，特斯拉的品牌价值蒸发了 150 亿美元。摩根士丹利对投资者的调查发现，85% 的受访者认为马斯克的政治关系对特斯拉产生了负面影响，40% 的人认为影响”极为负面”。

The competitive gap with BYD is real but requires more precision than the headline numbers suggest. BYD manufactures approximately 75% of its components in-house — batteries, power electronics, motors, semiconductors — giving it a battery pack cost advantage estimated at 20–25% against traditional manufacturers. Tesla competes on software and manufacturing efficiency (Gigacasting), not supply chain depth. BYD’s H1 2025 revenue of $51.9 billion surpassed Tesla’s $41.8 billion for the first time.

But BYD is not an unstoppable machine. China’s EV price war is crushing its margins too. Q2 2025 net profit fell 29.9% despite revenue growing 14% — BYD slashed prices by up to 34% on 22 models to defend market share. Gross margin fell from 22% in 2024 to roughly 20%, with some models at 14–16%. Inventory has ballooned to 154 billion RMB, with days-on-hand at 70–80 days versus an industry norm of 30–45. BYD’s R&D spending exceeded its net profit in 2025 — massive forward investment, but it means earnings are thin relative to revenue. In Europe, BYD faces a 27% combined EU tariff on Chinese-made BEVs (10% base + 17% anti-subsidy duty), which is why it’s pivoting to PHEVs and building local plants in Hungary and Turkey to manufacture outside the tariff wall. The European expansion is a genuine strategic counter — but it is expensive, complicated, and not yet at scale. BYD’s ADAS and software stack also remain behind Tesla’s.

与比亚迪的差距确实存在，但标题数字掩盖了不少细节。比亚迪约 75% 的零部件自产，涵盖电池、电力电子、电机和半导体，电池包成本比传统车企低估计 20%—25%。特斯拉的优势在软件和制造工艺（一体压铸）。2025 年上半年，比亚迪营收 519 亿美元，首次超越特斯拉的 418 亿美元。但比亚迪并非一台势不可挡的机器——中国的电动车价格战同样在侵蚀它的利润。2025 年第二季度，比亚迪净利润下降了 29.9%，尽管营收增长了 14%；为守住市场份额，它对 22 款车型降价最高达 34%。毛利率从 2024 年的 22% 降至约 20%，部分车型只有 14%—16%。库存飙升至 1540 亿元人民币，库存周转天数达 70—80 天，而行业正常水平是 30—45 天。2025 年，比亚迪的研发支出超过了净利润——大量前置投入，但这意味着相对营收而言利润极薄。在欧洲，比亚迪面临 27% 的综合关税（10% 基础关税加 17% 反补贴税），因此它正在转向插混车型，并在匈牙利和土耳其建厂以绕开关税壁垒。欧洲扩张在战略上有分量——但代价高昂、过程复杂，且尚未形成规模。比亚迪的智能驾驶和软件栈也仍落后于特斯拉。

The Structural Dependency

结构性依赖

The dependency is simple: the future bets need the present business to keep generating cash while they develop. The question is whether the timing works.

这种依赖关系很简单：未来的赌注需要现有业务在发展期间持续产生现金。问题在于时机是否能对上。

The $44 billion cash position buys time — roughly two to three years of negative free cash flow at the projected 2026 rate, if EV revenue stays flat. But EV revenue is not staying flat; it is declining. And the timeline for Cybercab to generate meaningful revenue requires regulatory approval that does not yet exist, a production ramp that is just beginning, and a network buildout that has no precedent at scale. Optimus has no external revenue timeline before late 2026 at the earliest, and commercial customers at scale are a 2027–2028 story at best.

440 亿美元的现金储备提供了时间缓冲——按 2026 年的预计速率，如果电动车收入保持不变，大约可以支撑两到三年的负自由现金流。但电动车收入并非保持不变，而是在下滑。而且，Cybercab 产生可观收入的时间表需要尚不存在的监管批准、刚刚开始的产能爬坡，以及没有先例的规模化网络建设。Optimus 最早在 2026 年底之前没有外部收入时间表，规模化商业客户最乐观也是 2027—2028 年的故事。

The window is 2026 to 2028. In that window, automotive cash generation needs to stay sufficient to fund the capex plan — or the $44 billion cash position needs to absorb the shortfall. Either the bets start producing before the cow runs dry, or Tesla faces a dilutive capital raise at a moment when the narrative is most vulnerable.

这个窗口期是 2026 年至 2028 年。在这个窗口期内，汽车业务产生的现金需要足以支撑资本支出计划——否则 440 亿美元的现金储备需要填补缺口。要么赌注在资金耗尽之前开始产出，要么特斯拉在叙事最脆弱的时刻面临摊薄性融资。

The Honest Bull Case

诚实的多头论点

The bear case above reflects the financial trajectory. The bull case has a technical foundation that is more concrete than it was a year ago. FSD v14, released in October 2025, is a measurable step change: the critical intervention rate improved from one per 217 miles (v13.2) to one per 834 miles (v14) in city driving — a fourfold improvement in a single year. Austin’s live unsupervised public service, however embryonic, is real operation in the world. The 8.2 billion cumulative FSD miles represent a training corpus that Waymo, operating in geofenced LIDAR-mapped environments, structurally cannot replicate — Waymo’s approach trades generalization for reliability. If the v13-to-v14 improvement rate continues into v15 and beyond, the capability gap with Waymo narrows. When it closes enough to satisfy regulators at scale, the cash flow picture reverses quickly. The energy segment — Megapack (utility-scale grid battery storage), Powerwall (residential), and solar — is also a real business that trades at a discount inside the Tesla narrative. $12.8 billion in revenue, ~30% gross margin, 27% annual growth, and generating 23% of Tesla’s total profit while representing only 13% of revenue. Megapack is the main story: containerized grid-scale batteries sold to utilities for peak shaving and renewable output smoothing. This is a structurally different market from EVs, growing independently, and the market prices it as an appendage to the car business.

上述空头逻辑反映的是财务走势。多头论点有一个比一年前更扎实的技术基础。2025 年 10 月发布的 FSD v14 是一次可量化的跨越：城市驾驶中关键干预间隔从 v13.2 的每 217 英里一次提升至 v14 的每 834 英里一次——一年内提升了四倍。奥斯汀的无监督公开服务，无论多么初步，都已经在公共道路上实际运营了。82 亿英里的累计 FSD 里程代表着一个 Waymo 在结构上无法复制的训练语料库——Waymo 在地理围栏的激光雷达地图环境中运行，用泛化能力换可靠性。如果 v13 到 v14 的改进曲线在 v15 及之后延续，与 Waymo 的能力差距将持续收窄。当差距缩小到足以满足监管要求并规模落地时，现金流图景会迅速翻转。能源业务——Megapack（公用事业级电网储能）、Powerwall（家用储能）和太阳能——同样是一门被特斯拉整体叙事遮蔽了的实在生意。128 亿美元收入，约 30% 毛利率，27% 年增速，贡献了特斯拉总利润的 23%，却只占营收的 13%。Megapack 是核心增长点：集装箱式电网级电池，卖给电力公司用于削峰填谷和平滑可再生能源出力。这是一个与汽车完全不同的市场，独立增长，但市场把它定价成了汽车业务的附属品。

The Conclusion

结论

Tesla’s stock is priced on the robotaxi and Optimus thesis. The EV business — which is what actually exists and generates cash today — is declining on every volume and margin metric. The 2026 capex acceleration creates a cash burn inflection that the $44 billion cushion can absorb, but not indefinitely. The question is not whether the bets are real. They are. The question is whether the window stays open long enough.

特斯拉的股价定价在机器人出租车和 Optimus 的叙事上。电动车业务——也就是今天实际存在并产生现金的东西——在每一项销量和利润率指标上都在下滑。2026 年的资本支出加速制造了一个现金消耗的拐点，440 亿美元的缓冲可以吸收，但不是无限期的。赌注本身不是问题——这些事正在发生。问题是窗口期能不能撑得够久。
