Meta: The One Getting the Balance Right

发表于 2026-02-28 更新于 2026-03-01 阅读次数：

This is part of a series on the Magnificent Seven. The framing — four distinct scenarios, seven companies that should not be priced as one story — is laid out in the original memo.

本文是科技七巨头系列的一部分。四种截然不同的终局剧本、不应被当作同一个故事定价的七家公司——完整框架见原始备忘录。

The market narrative on Meta reads like this: massive capex, uncertain AI ROI, regulatory overhang, and a CEO who built the metaverse bonfire. The stock is accordingly priced at roughly 22x forward earnings — cheaper than Google, cheaper than Microsoft, cheaper than Nvidia. That discount is a mistake.

市场对 Meta 的叙事大概是这样的：资本支出庞大、AI 回报不确定、监管阴云笼罩，还有一个曾经亲手点燃元宇宙篝火的 CEO。股价因此被定在约 22 倍的远期市盈率——比谷歌便宜，比微软便宜，比英伟达也便宜。这个折价定错了。

The Surface Reading

表面的叙事

Meta spent $72 billion on capex in 2025. It’s guiding $115–135 billion for 2026 — nearly double. That’s the number that gets quoted. What doesn’t get quoted: Meta expects operating income in 2026 to exceed 2025 operating income, despite doubling the spend. No other hyperscaler is making that claim.

Meta 在 2025 年的资本支出为 720 亿美元，2026 年的指引是 1150 亿至 1350 亿——几乎翻倍。这个数字被反复引用。但有一个数字没有被引用：Meta 预期 2026 年的营业利润将超过 2025 年，尽管支出几乎翻了一番。其他超大规模云厂商没有一家做出这种承诺。

The reason is partly structural. Microsoft’s AI investment creates a product called Copilot, which needs enterprise buyers willing to pay $30/seat/month — a separate conversion step. Google also directly monetizes AI through its ads platform (Performance Max, Smart Bidding, AI Max), same as Meta. But Google faces an additional burden Meta doesn’t: the query paradigm itself is under threat. If AI assistants handle more requests that previously went through search, Google’s ad inventory shrinks at the source. Meta’s social engagement model has no equivalent structural threat. AI doesn’t replace scrolling a social feed the way it might replace entering a search query. So Meta’s AI investment is purely offensive; Google’s is simultaneously offensive and defensive.

原因部分来自结构。微软的 Copilot 需要企业买家单独付费——是多出来的一步转化。谷歌也通过广告平台直接变现 AI（Performance Max、Smart Bidding、AI Max），与 Meta 的 Advantage+ 在结构上相同。但谷歌额外承受着一个 Meta 没有的负担：查询范式本身在受威胁。如果 AI 助手承接了越来越多原本通过搜索处理的请求，谷歌的广告库存就会在源头萎缩。Meta 的社交互动模式没有等价的结构性威胁——AI 不会像替代搜索查询那样替代刷信息流这个行为。所以 Meta 的 AI 投入是纯进攻型的；谷歌的同时兼顾进攻和防守。

Where the ROI Is Already Closing

ROI 已经在兑现的地方

Meta’s AI investment runs directly into its existing ad machine. The mechanism: AI improves targeting accuracy, which improves conversion rates, which increases advertisers’ willingness to pay. AI also automates creative generation and testing at scale, compressing the production cost of running effective campaigns. Ad impressions rose 18% in Q4 2025; average price per ad rose 6%.

Meta 的 AI 投资直接注入广告系统。定向精度提升了，广告主愿意出的价随之提高；创意的生成和测试实现了大规模自动化，有效广告的制作成本被压下来——这些改进在竞价拍卖里直接变现，不需要额外的转化步骤。2025 年第四季度，广告展示量增长 18%，广告均价上涨 6%。

Isolating AI’s contribution here is genuinely hard, and the honest answer is that multiple things drove Meta’s 24% Q4 2025 ad revenue growth. TikTok’s January 2025 outage drove a measurable spike: Meta ad volume rose 6.3% and spend rose 22.4% from displaced TikTok budgets in that window alone. 8 of TikTok’s top 10 advertiser categories cut spend in Q1 2025. If TikTok is permanently removed from the U.S., Meta is projected to absorb 55% of the displaced ad revenue. That is a structural tailwind from regulatory disruption, not AI. Reels monetization maturation is another. The improving digital ad market overall is a third. Meta’s 24% growth versus Google’s 13% is partly explained by Meta being the primary beneficiary of TikTok ad spend rotation — not a factor Google benefits from equivalently.

把 AI 的具体贡献拆出来，说实话很难，诚实的答案是多重因素共同推动了 Meta 2025 年第四季度 24% 的广告收入增长。TikTok 2025 年 1 月的关闭风波制造了一个可量化的脉冲：仅那段时间，Meta 的广告投放量上涨 6.3%，广告支出上涨 22.4%，来自被迫转移的 TikTok 预算。TikTok 前十大广告主类别中有 8 个在 2025 年第一季度削减了支出。如果 TikTok 在美国被永久下架，Meta 预计将吸收 55% 的流失广告收入。这是来自监管混乱的结构性顺风，与 AI 无关。Reels 货币化的成熟是另一个因素，整体数字广告市场的回暖是第三个。Meta 24% 对谷歌 13% 的增长差距，部分来自 Meta 是 TikTok 广告预算转移的主要受益方——这不是谷歌同等受益的因素。

The Llama Bet

Llama 的赌注

The strategic logic of open-sourcing Llama is real: if developers build on your model, you embed your assumptions into their applications without selling a subscription. Give away the OS, influence the ecosystem. Llama 2 and 3 built genuine momentum — the model library at its peak accumulated hundreds of millions of downloads.

开源 Llama 背后的逻辑站得住脚：开发者在你的模型上构建，你就把自己的技术假设嵌进了他们的应用，不用卖订阅服务。Llama 2 和 3 在这条路上走出了势头——下载量巅峰时累计以亿计。

The execution has gone sideways. Llama 4, released in April 2025, was a benchmark controversy — Meta submitted a non-public “experimental” version to the Chatbot Arena leaderboard; the model actually released to developers came last on coding benchmarks. More consequentially: Alibaba’s Qwen series overtook Llama in HuggingFace downloads by late 2025. By early 2026, Qwen is the more commonly used base model for fine-tuning in the open-source community. The “default open model” position — which was the whole point of the strategy — is currently Qwen’s, not Llama’s.

执行层面出了问题。2025 年 4 月发布的 Llama 4 陷入基准测试争议，Meta 提交给排行榜的是非公开的”实验版”，真正发布的版本在代码基准上排名垫底。更关键的是：阿里巴巴的 Qwen 系列在 2025 年底的 Hugging Face 下载量超过了 Llama，到 2026 年初，Qwen 已经是开源社区中更常用的微调基础模型。”开源默认标准”这个位置——这恰恰是整个策略的核心——目前属于 Qwen，不属于 Llama。

The strategic direction remains correct. The execution, for now, is losing. Whether Llama recovers depends on what comes next.

战略方向依然正确，执行层面暂时在输。能否翻盘，取决于接下来的动作。

The 1 Billion Monthly Users (And What They Actually Do)

十亿月活用户（以及他们实际在做什么）

Meta AI crossed 1 billion monthly active users in May 2025. The headline sounds impressive; the daily engagement figure is more honest — roughly 40 million daily users, 185 million weekly. That’s a 4% daily-to-monthly ratio, which means most users who’ve tried Meta AI don’t return every day. The distribution reach is real; the depth of habit formation is not yet demonstrated.

Meta AI 于 2025 年 5 月突破月活十亿。这个标题数字听起来不错，但日活数字更诚实——大约 4000 万日活用户，1.85 亿周活用户。月活换算成日活只有 4%，说明大多数试用过 Meta AI 的用户并未养成日常习惯。渠道把用户送到了门口，但每天回来是另一回事。

The more significant datapoint is where AI is converting on the ad side. By Q3 2025, Meta’s end-to-end automated ad solutions — which handle targeting, placement, bidding, and creative — reached $60 billion in annualized revenue run rate. This is direct, measurable monetization of AI spend into the ad system — not a retention argument, but an efficiency one.

更关键的数据在广告侧。截至 2025 年第三季度，Meta 的端到端自动化广告解决方案——涵盖定向、投放、竞价和创意——年化收入规模已达 600 亿美元。这不是用户留存的数字，而是效率提升的直接账单。

The Honest Case Against

诚实的反驳

The margin trajectory is real. Operating margin fell from 48% in Q4 2024 to 41% in Q4 2025 as costs and expenses rose 40% year-on-year. If capex doubles again in 2026 without proportional revenue acceleration, that compression continues. The promise of higher 2026 operating income than 2025 is guidance, not fact. Zuckerberg has been wrong about big bets before — the metaverse consumed $40+ billion and produced a declining Reality Labs segment.

利润率的压缩看得见。随着成本和费用同比增长 40%，营业利润率从 2024 年第四季度的 48% 下降至 2025 年第四季度的 41%。如果 2026 年资本支出再次翻倍但收入增长没有成比例加速，这种压缩将持续。2026 年营业利润高于 2025 年的承诺只是指引，不是事实。扎克伯格在大赌注上曾经出错过——元宇宙消耗了超过 400 亿美元，产出的是一个持续亏损的 Reality Labs 部门。

The bull case requires believing that this time the allocation decision is right. There’s a reasonable argument that it is: AI improving ad targeting has immediate measurable feedback loops, unlike metaverse VR headset speculation. But the execution risk is real.

看多需要相信这次的钱花对了。有一个可以辩护的理由：AI 在广告定向上的回报可以即时量化，不像元宇宙那样把钱烧进了一个假设里。但方向正确不等于执行能跟上。

The Conclusion

结论

The actual Meta case is this: its core ad business is growing at 24% versus Google’s 13%, with no structural threat to the underlying behavior (social feed scrolling) that generates that inventory. Google is fighting on two fronts — defending Search from AI disruption while building Gemini as an offensive product. Microsoft needs Copilot adoption to convert. Apple has no visible AI product. Meta’s AI spend is entirely offensive, pointed at an existing revenue machine that’s already responding. At 22x forward earnings versus Google at 27x, the market is pricing in the metaverse scar tissue without adequately pricing that asymmetry.

Meta 的实际论点是这样的：其核心广告业务以 24% 的速度增长，而谷歌只有 13%，且没有任何结构性威胁指向产生这些广告库存的底层行为（刷信息流）。谷歌在两条战线上作战——既要防守搜索免受 AI 颠覆，又要进攻性地构建 Gemini。微软需要 Copilot 的企业端转化。苹果没有可见的 AI 产品突破。Meta 的 AI 投入完全是进攻型的，指向一台已经在响应的现有收入机器。以 22 倍远期市盈率对比谷歌的 27 倍，市场把元宇宙的历史疤痕定进了价格，却没有充分定价这种不对称性。

The Llama open-source bet is the right strategic concept and the wrong execution so far. Qwen holds the default position Llama was supposed to occupy. That’s a real vulnerability, and it has no obvious near-term fix.

Llama 开源赌注，方向正确，执行在输。Qwen 占据了 Llama 本应占据的位置，这是真实的弱点，短期内没有明显的解法。
